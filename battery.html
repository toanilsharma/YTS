Industrial Battery Types
Vented Lead-Acid (LA 2V)
20-25yr life. High maintenance (needs water). EODV: ~1.75V/cell.
VRLA (SMF 2V / 12V)
10-12yr life. Maintenance-free. Must be in cool, clean room.
Nickel-Cadmium (Ni-Cd 1.2V)
25+ yr life. Loves high temps. Different EODV: ~1.1V/cell.
