How to "Zero" the DP
(The #1 Task)
1. CLOSE both Block Valves (HP & LP).
2. OPEN the Equalizing Valve.
(DP = 0).
3. Perform "Zero" at transmitter.
4. CLOSE Equalizer *firmly*.
5. SLOWLY open HP, then LP block.

CRITICAL: Impulse Lines
(Wet Leg vs. Dry Leg)
LIQUIDS / STEAM (Wet Leg):
Mount transmitter BELOW. Slope lines down to TX. (No gas pockets).
GASES (Dry Leg):
Mount transmitter ABOVE. Slope lines down to *process*. (No liquid pockets).

Problem:
