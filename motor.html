The Environment

IP55 (Dust/Water) = Standard
TEFC
(Totally Enclosed Fan Cooled)
Hazardous Area
Is it Zone 1, 2, 21, or 22?
Ex d / Ex e

Ambient & Altitude

STEP 4
Performance DNA
Efficiency (IEC 60034-30)
IE3 Premium
The modern standard for OpEx.
IE4 Super Premium
Lowest Total Cost of Ownership.
Insulation Class
Class F (155°C)
...with Class B (130°C) Rise.
This "thermal margin" is the key to motor longevity.
NEMA Design
Defines Torque Curve
B (Std) or C (High Start)
Crucial for hard-to-start loads.

STEP 5
The Start-Up
Starting Method
DOL? Star-Delta? Soft Starter?
VFD / Inverter Duty?
A VFD-Rated motor is NOT optional.
Key Torque Data
Locked Rotor Torque (LRT)
Breakdown Torque (BDT)
Motor torque MUST exceed load torque at all speeds.
Starts per Hour
How often will it start?
Too many starts will overheat and destroy the motor.
