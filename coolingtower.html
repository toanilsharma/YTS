The "OpEx Killer"
Total Makeup Water
Makeup = Evap + Blowdown + Drift
Evaporation: The cooling! ~1% of flow per 5.5¬∞C Range.
Blowdown: Draining water to control mineral (TDS) buildup.
Drift: Mist lost to air (small with good drift eliminators).
This is your 24/7 water bill!

Expert Tip: "COC"
Cycles of Concentration
What is it?
Measures how "concentrated" the minerals are in your tower vs. makeup water.
e.g., 3 COC = 3x the minerals
Why it Matters:
Higher COC = LESS Blowdown = Less Water Wasted.
...but requires better chemical treatment to prevent scale.

STEP 4: Choose Draft
How the Air Moves
Forced Draft
Fan at bottom, BLOWING air IN. High velocity, but can re-circulate air.
Induced Draft
Fan at top, SUCKING air OUT. Most common & efficient. Good discharge velocity.

STEP 5: Choose Flow
Air vs. Water Path
Cross-Flow
Air moves HORIZONTALLY through the fill as water falls.
Counter-Flow
Air moves VERTICALLY up, against the falling water. MOST EFFICIENT.
