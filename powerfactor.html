STEP 4: The Solution
APFC Relay
Your plant load changes. Your kVAR bank should too.
APFC Relay
An "Automatic Power Factor Controller" is a brain. It switches capacitor "stages" on/off to perfectly match the load.
It always maintains your 0.99 target and prevents over-correction.

STEP 5
Expert Choice: Switching
Contactor Switching
Slower (5-30s), mechanical, audible "clunk".
Perfect for stable loads (fans, pumps) or main incomers.
Thyristor (Static)
Instant (<20ms), silent, electronic. No moving parts.
MANDATORY for dynamic loads like welders, cranes, or lifts.

STEP 7: The Pro Solution
Detuned Reactors
If you have VFDs (>15% of total load), you MUST use detuned banks.
Detuned Reactor
A "reactor" (inductor) is added in series with each capacitor stage.
This blocks the harmonic currents, protecting the capacitor and preventing resonance.
