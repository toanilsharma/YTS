Law 1: Flow (Q)
Flow is proportional to Speed
Flow (Q)
Q_new = Q_old × (N_new / N_old)
Example
80% Speed (N)
= 80% Flow (Q)
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
Law 2: Head (H)
Head is proportional to Speed SQUARED
Head (H)
H_new = H_old × (N_new / N_old)²
Example
80% Speed (N)
= 64% Head (H)
(0.8 × 0.8 = 0.64)
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
Law 3: Power (P)
Power is proportional to Speed CUBED
Power (P)
P_new = P_old × (N_new / N_old)³
Example
80% Speed (N)
= 51.2% Power (P)
(0.8 × 0.8 × 0.8 = 0.512)

Critical Caveats
When to be careful...
1. Friction Dominated ONLY.

2. Centrifugal Pumps ONLY.
(NEVER for Positive Displacement pumps!)
3. Check Min. Speed.
(For motor cooling & pump stability)
