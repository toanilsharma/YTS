THE #1 MISTAKE
Ignoring Choked Flow
FLASHING
If P2 < Pv (Vapor Pressure), liquid boils and STAYS vapor. Destroys trim.
CAVITATION
Liquid boils at the valve, then collapses back to liquid. Extreme damage.

Step 3: Selection
"The 30-80% Rule"
The Goal
Your *calculated* Cv (at normal flow) should be 30% to 80% of the *valve's* rated Cv.
Oversizing is BAD
A valve running at < 10% open has poor control and high wear. The smallest valve that works is the BEST valve.

Dos & Don'ts (Safety)
Installation & Safety
DO: Define Fail-Safe Mode (Fail Open or Fail Close).
DO: Install upstream strainer/filter.
DO: Install block & bypass valves.
DO: Allow 5-10 pipe diameters of straight run.
DON'T: Ignore noise (it's cavitation or high velocity).
