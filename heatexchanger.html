What Is It?
The "Plant's Radiator"
A Heat Exchanger just moves heat (energy) from one fluid to another.
e.g., Hot Oil heats up Cold Water
LMTD
Is how we calculate the "driving force" (average temperature difference) that moves the heat.

The OpEx Killer
Fouling Factor (Rf)
What is it?
Over time, "crud" builds up on the tubes, insulating them and stopping heat transfer.
What it means:
You MUST add a "Fouling Factor" to your calculation. This makes your exchanger LARGER (more 'A') on Day 1, so it still works on Day 1000.
