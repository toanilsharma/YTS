Expert Cal #1
"Zero ELEVATION"
Problem:
Transmitter is 1m BELOW the 0% tank tap.
Solution:
This 1m of liquid head *elevates* the zero.
LRV (4mA) = +Head (e.g., +100 mbar)
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
Expert Cal #2
"Zero SUPPRESSION"
Problem: (Wet Leg)
The LP tap on a steam drum *fills with water*. This false head must be removed.
Solution:
Calibrate to *suppress* this head.
LRV (4mA) = -Head (e.g., -500 mbar)

How to "Zero" (Gauge)
(Open Tank / Pressure)
1. CLOSE Block Valve.
2. SLOWLY open Bleed Valve to vent.
(Pressure is now 0 barg).
3. Perform "Zero" at transmitter.
4. CLOSE Bleed, SLOWLY open Block.
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
How to "Zero" (DP)
(Closed Tank Level)
1. CLOSE both Block Valves (HP & LP).
2. OPEN the Equalizing Valve.
(DP = 0).
3. Perform "Zero" at transmitter.
4. CLOSE Equalizer *firmly*.
5. SLOWLY open HP, then LP block.
