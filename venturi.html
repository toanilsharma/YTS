Step 2: Sizing (β)
The Core Design Choice
β = d / D
(Throat Diameter ÷ Pipe ID)
ISO 5167 Limits
Venturi: 0.3 < β < 0.75
Nozzle: 0.2 < β < 0.8

Expert Check (Gases)
Apply Expansibility (Y)
Gases Expand
As pressure drops, gas expands, changing its density. You MUST correct for this using the (Y or ε) factor.
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
CRITICAL: Installation
(The Venturi Advantage)
DO: Use a Venturi to reduce straight pipe runs. (Less sensitive to swirl).
DO: Weld nozzles carefully, ensuring no protrusion.
DON'T: Assume "zero" straight run. You still need some!
prepared by https://designcalculators.co.in
CRITICAL: Tap Mounting
(Impulse Lines)
LIQUIDS / STEAM:
Mount transmitter BELOW taps. (Keeps lines full of liquid).
GASES:
Mount transmitter ABOVE taps. (Allows condensate to drain).
