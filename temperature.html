The "Absolute" Scale
Kelvin (K)
Why Kelvin?
0 K = Absolute Zero.
(The point of zero molecular energy).
No negative numbers. This makes gas laws and physics calculations possible.
Note: It's "Kelvin", not "degrees Kelvin".
